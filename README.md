## ✨ Overview

CustomerPulse is a modern Power BI dashboard designed to provide a 360° view of customer performance and business growth.

The dashboard enables organizations to analyze customer behavior, monitor retention and churn, evaluate revenue trends, identify high-value customers, and uncover actionable insights through interactive visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Metrics

| KPI | Description |
|------|------------|
| 💰 Total Revenue | Track overall business performance |
| 👥 Active Customers | Monitor customer engagement |
| 💎 Customer Lifetime Value | Measure long-term customer value |
| 📈 Revenue Growth | Analyze growth trends |
| 🔄 Retention Rate | Evaluate customer loyalty |
| 📉 Churn Rate | Identify customer attrition |
